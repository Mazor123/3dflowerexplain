ROSE

Roses are one of the most popular and recognizable flowers in the world. Known for their beauty and fragrance, roses have a rich history and a variety of uses and meanings

MARIGOLDS

Marigolds, known for their bright, cheerful flowers, are popular in gardens and landscapes around the world. They belong to the genus Tagetes and are prized for their vibrant colors, ease of growth, and various uses

TULIP

Tulips symbolize perfect love and rebirth. They are often associated with spring and new beginnings.

LILY

Lilies represent purity, motherhood, and renewal. White lilies are particularly associated with purity and funerals.

LAVINDER

Lavender symbolizes calm, grace, and serenity.
